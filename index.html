About

Athul PR is a sound professional working in film production, commercials, and audio post-production based in Kerala, India.

His work includes location sound recording, sound design, FX recording, and audio post for feature films, short films, and advertising projects. He has collaborated with film directors and production teams across Malayalam cinema and independent filmmaking.

On set, Athul captures clean dialogue and atmosphere using professional Sound Devices recorders and Sennheiser wireless systems. In post, he delivers immersive audio mixes and designed soundscapes for festival films, commercial productions, and digital platforms.
